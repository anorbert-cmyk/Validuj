Methodology

How the six-agent Validuj workflow works

Instead of one oversized prompt, Validuj uses a structured chain of specialists. Each stage produces a compact handoff for the next stage, which reduces context bloat and keeps the analysis focused.

1. Market Scout

Frames the problem, target customer, urgency, pain points, and evidence of demand using live search enrichment.

2. Competitor Analyst

Maps competitors, substitutes, and whitespace opportunities using the market handoff from stage 1.

3. Strategy Architect

Turns market and competition findings into positioning, roadmap priorities, and go-to-market direction.

4. Product Designer

Describes the MVP scope, main workflow, information architecture, and product experience design.

5. Edge-Case Reviewer

Stress-tests operational edge cases, exception paths, and trust or safety concerns.

6. Risk & Decision Analyst

Summarizes risks, proposes validation experiments, and issues a practical go / no-go recommendation.

Why handoff matters

The system saves only compact structured state between stages. That means later agents inherit the most important findings without dragging forward every earlier paragraph.

Why SEO is included

Validuj exposes discoverable public product pages and a demo report while marking private run pages as non-indexable. This keeps the product crawlable without leaking user-specific analysis pages.
